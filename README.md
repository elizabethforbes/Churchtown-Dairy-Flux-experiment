# Churchtown-Dairy-Flux-experiment
GHG flux experiment after variable manure fertilizer application, 2025 growing season

Data: 
flux data for CO2, CH4, N20 taken over the course of the growing season from early May (before application) until October (end of growing season). Data taken every two weeks except immediately before and after manure subsidy application on experimental plots (May 28th, 2025).
aboveground biomass data from a 0.5m2 quadrat marked off at the corner of each experimental plot
